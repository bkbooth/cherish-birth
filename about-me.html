I have a husband Ben who I adore. A daughter Amilia who I absolutely treasure. I have a passion to see people loved, cherished and gain a sense of belonging. I live in one of the most beautiful parts of the world, The Illawarra, NSW.

Why I became a Doula

I have always had a passion for mums and babies, but I think it became even stronger after the birth of my daughter. I was shocked at how disconnected I felt from my care providers and how much information I had to source for myself that really should have been coming from them. I had a beautiful birth, but it made me wonder, are other women getting enough information to have a birth experience that they are at peace with?
